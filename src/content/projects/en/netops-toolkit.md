# NetOps Toolkit

A collection of essential tools for network engineers and administrators, focused on usability and clear visualization of complex data.

## Included Tools

- **Visual VLSM Calculator**: Design subnets and view graphical IP occupation.
- **Unit Converter**: Quick utilities for bandwidth and storage.
- **Quick Reference**: Cheat-sheets for common protocols and ports.

## What makes it special?

It transforms dry and error-prone network calculations into a **visual and interactive** experience. Unlike traditional text-based calculators, this tool offers immediate graphical representation of network distribution, facilitating comprehension and planning for network engineers.

## Technologies

Developed with standard web technologies to ensure compatibility and performance, deployed globally on **Netlify**.
