# MedalBlog

This is my personal blog, designed to be a fast and accessible space where I share my learnings and software development tutorials.

## Key Features

- **Custom CMS**: Markdown content management for seamless writing.
- **Continuous Deployment**: Hosted on Vercel for maximum speed and global availability.
- **Optimized SEO**: Technical structure prepared for indexing and visibility.

## What makes it special?

More than just a blog, it's a personal branding platform built from scratch. It integrates a **minimalist and accessible** design with exceptional performance (Lighthouse 100/100), demonstrating how a content site can be visually appealing without sacrificing speed.

## Technologies

Developed using a modern stack that ensures performance and maintainability.
